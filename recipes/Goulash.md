Goulash
=======

Ingredients
-----------

- 1 lb ground beef
- 1 can vegetable soup
- 1/2 cup water
- 1/4 tsp garlic salt
- 1 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper
- 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
- 1/2 tsp dry mustard
- 8 oz package elbow macaroni, cooked

Directions
----------

1. Brown beef.
2. Add can of vegetable soup and water.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Add remaining ingredients.
5. Bake at 350F for 20 minutes.
