Fish tacos
===========

Ingredients
-----------

- 2lbs tilapia
- Corn tortillas
- Cilantro
- Onion
- Lime
- Chili powder
- Salt
- Oil


Directions
----------

1. Fry tilapia in oil and a heavy coating of chili powder. Toward end of cooking, gently break up filets into bite-size chunks
2. Fry/steam tortillas
3. Serve
