Pork chops and rice
===================

Ingredients
-----------

- 6 pork chops
- salt and pepper
- oil
- 1 cup rice
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup milk

Directions
----------

1. Salt and pepper the pork chops, and brown in oil in frying pan.
2. Place in 9x13" baking dish. Cover w/ rice, soup, milk.
3. Cover w/ foil, bake at 350F for 1:15.
