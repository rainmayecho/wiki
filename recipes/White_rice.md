White rice
==========

Ingredients
-----------

- 2 cups rice
- Water
    - 2 cups water for Ricelands Best
    - 2.25 cups water for Botan
    - 3 cups water for basmati
- Rice:water ratio is around 2:3 by volume. Slightly less water higher batch size.

Directions
----------

1. Rinse
2. Rice cooker
3. Boom
