Susie salad
===========

Ingredients
-----------

- 2 stalks uncooked broccoli
- 1 bunch cauliflower
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 4oz shredded cheddar cheese
- 3-4 strips bacon, chopped
- Sunflower seeds
- Raisins
- 1/2 cup sugar
- 1 cup mayo
- 4 Tbsp vinegar

Directions
----------

1. Combine dry ingredients. Combine wet ingredients. Combine wet and dry before serving.
