Fried green tomatoes
====================

Ingredients
-----------

- Milk coating
    - 1/4 cup milk
    - 2 eggs
- Flour coating
    - 3/4 cup flour
    - 1/4 cup cornstarch
    - 1 T cayenne pepper
    - 1 T black pepper
- Panko breadcrumbs
- Oil

Directions
----------

1. Milk -> flour coating -> milk -> breadcrumbs
2. Fry in a pan of oil
